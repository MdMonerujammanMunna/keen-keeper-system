# 🌟 KeenKeeper

A Modern Friendship & Relationship Analytics & Communication Platform

🚀 Live Demo:[Click here](https://keenkeepercommunicationsystem.netlify.app/)  

---

## 📌 Overview

KeenKeeper is a modern web application designed to help you understand, track, and strengthen communication in your most important relationships.

---

## ❓ Why KeenKeeper?

Stay in control of your relationships.

Identify friends you’re close to.

Strengthen your closest bonds.

Communicate easily with everyone.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📊 Friendship Analytics

* Beautiful pie chart for timeline distribution

* Instantly see your full social circle

### 📅 Interactive Timeline

* Full chronological history of your interactions

* Track calls, messages, meetings & moments

* Clean and simple activity log

### 🎨 Modern UI/UX

* Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, and desktop)

* Beautiful cards with smooth navigation

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
